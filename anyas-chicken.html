Anya's Chicken
  • Ingredients:
  • 6 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon of Crushed Garlic
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Method:
  • Chop the chicken into chunks and place it in a bowl
  • Add crushed garlic and a glug of soy sauce (1/2 table spoons)
  • Cover and chill in the fridge for a couple of hours
  • When ready to cook crack the egg into the bowl and stir to coat the chicken
  • On a plate mix flower and cornflower in equal measure
  • Take each bit of chicken and roll in the flower mixture
  • Pop into frying pan with hot oil and cook until crispy (turning regularly)
